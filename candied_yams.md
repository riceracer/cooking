# Candied Yams

## Ingredients

* 3 large red/purple fleshed yams
* salt
* paprika
* 1 tsp cinnamon
* 1/2 tsp powdered ginger (optional)
* 3/4 cup brown sugar
* 1 tbsp fresh squeezed lemon juice
* 1 tbsp fresh squeezed orange juice
* 1 tsp grated lemon zest
* 1 tsp grated orange zest
* 2 tbsp melted butter
* mini marshmallows (optional topping)

## Steps

1. Cut yams to fit in boiling pan, boil until just tender when poking with fork (~20 min)
2. Drain yams, peel, cut into 1/2 - 1 in pieces and put in a 8x8 to 9x13 baking pan.
3. Season with spices.
4. Sprinkle / cover with sugar, lemon and orange juice.
5. Spread light amounts over lemon and orange zest over yams.
6. Dot with butter.
7. Top with marshmallows or 1/2 cover of marshmallows.
8. Bake at 375 F, until top is glazed.
